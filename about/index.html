About Us

Know More About Us

Quality in Everything, The Best You Can Get!

Iconix Technologies is an independent locally owned company, established as a system integrator company in 2008 in the Kathmandu of Nepal. The company is managed by a professional and highly experienced team focusing on fulfilling the technical and IT needs of major governmental, industrial and commercial potential of Nepal. Iconix has almost two decades of experience delivering hardware & service integration services to clients across Nepal, in both the public and private sectors. Our mission, vision, goal and motto are entirely dedicated towards a successful long-term partnership with customers aiming to be partners in progress. Our obligation is to provide the Nepalese market (Government & Corporate) with innovative technologies, Support and Services. It has been an exciting time to be involved with the industry and we are looking forward to the coming years and being able to provide systems and products to meet the demands of the future.
