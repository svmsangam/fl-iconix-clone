QA/Software Tester

No. of Vacancy/s : 1

Job Requirements:

  • Develop Test Scenario and Test Scripts based on User Stories
  • Perform Regression, Functional, Performance, Usability, and User Acceptance testing based on documented requirements
  • Manage and execute the Software Testing Life Cycle (STLS) – requirements analysis, test planning, test case development, environmental setup, test execution, test cycle closure
  • Ensures products, applications, and systems are in compliance with established quality standards, best practices for supportable systems
  • Work closely with IT teams to support testing strategies and activities. Ensure overall integrity of testing strategy and established acceptance test criteria
  • Review test scenarios for execution of testing to ensure adequacy of testing with compliance, system specifications that adequately perform for the business
  • Maintain library of test scripts for re-use across projects and as regression test beds
  • Build strong and effective communication with PM, developers and business clients
  • Ability to create test plans from business requirements, technical specifications or verbal communications
  • Prioritizes and uses time efficiently; develops realistic action plans
  • Troubleshoot software bugs and assist users with support tickets
  • Maintain understanding of new testing strategies and best practices

 

Qualifications

  • At least Bachelor’s degree in Computer Engineering or equivalent.
  • Proficient with Object Oriented Programming concepts
  • Good knowledge of a programming language - .NET (C#) or Django Framework (Python)
  • Highly organized and great attention to detail
  • Experience testing software in an agile environment
  • 2+ years of experience with technical troubleshooting, customer support, or account management
  • Experience with TDD and BDD concepts
  • Experience with code versioning using Git
  • Experience in Agile and Scrum a plus
  • Ability to provide accurate and thorough points-of-view
  • Intermediate experience writing SQL queries preferred

 

We Offer:

  • Exciting work in a unique industry: great opportunity for self-development in the sports industry
  • Opportunity to work on different projects covering many different sports
  • A fun, relaxed and stimulating modern working environment
  • Supportive, friendly, multinational, passionate team
  • Career opportunity in an international company
  • Your own workstation
  • Free Lunch
  • Participate in events
  • Continuous learning and development opportunities to work in the latest technology stacks
  • Flexible and casual work culture
  • Highly tenured mentors and engaged coworkers and partners
