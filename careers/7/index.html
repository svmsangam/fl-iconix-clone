Dot-NET Developer

No. of Vacancy/s : 1

Job Descriptions:

  • Leadership and ability to guide design and technical meetings
  • Excellent understanding of object-oriented design concepts and software development processes and methods
  • Expertise in application, data and infrastructure architecture disciplines
  • Advanced knowledge of architecture, design and business processes
  • Involve in designing and developing web services, desktop-based, and web-based software applications.
  • Attend daily stand-ups and other time-boxed meetings
  • Participate in the design phase of the application interface, application functionality, and database
  • Create ER Diagrams, implementing logical into physical database models from requirements.
  • Write  stored procedures, tune database and query
  • Participate in software production deployments
  • Perform production support efforts as directed tight-aggressive timelines.
  • Keep abreast with the latest developments in Microsoft .Net technologies; share knowledge with the team.
  • Develop automated tests and conduct performance tuning

 

Qualifications:

  • A bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or a related field is preferred
  • Experience working in an Agile development process
  • Experience with C#.Net and JavaScript of 3+ is a must.
  • Experience in MVC 5, Entity Framework, WPF, and JQuery is a must.
  • Experience with REST, JSON, XML, and Web Services development
  • Ability to write and troubleshoot TSQL code and design stored procedures, functions, tables, views, indexes and constraints.
  • Experience working with optimization and tuning a SQL Server database is a must.  
  • Solid understanding of object-oriented concepts, design patterns, and clean architecture.
  • Experience in source control tools like SVN, TFS, or GIT.
  • Hands-on experience with Project Management tools like Redmine, JIRA, or Trello.
  • High level of personal commitment to each task, a "Can do attitude" and a drive to deliver.
  • The candidate should be open to learning and working on new technologies as and when required
  • Strong communication skills to be able to communicate at all levels.
  • Working with API's

 

We Offer:

  • Exciting work in a unique industry: a great opportunity for self-development in the sports industry
  • Opportunity to work on different projects covering many different sports
  • A fun, relaxed and stimulating modern working environment
  • A supportive, friendly, multinational, passionate team
  • Career opportunity in an international company
  • Your own workstation
  • Free Lunch
  • Participate in events
  • Continuous learning and development opportunities to work in the latest technology stacks
  • Flexible and casual work culture
  • Highly tenured mentors and engaged coworkers and partners
